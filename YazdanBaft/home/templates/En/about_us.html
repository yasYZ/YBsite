About Yazdan Baft Company

Yazdan Baft Company started its activities in the field of production of all polymer derivatives, manufacturing plastic sacks (polypropylene) and wide plastic sheets (polyethylene), and weaving and sewing all kinds of industrial sacks such as jumbo bags, big bags, and bitumen bags. Due to the high quality of its products, Yazdan Baft has been able to satisfy its customers over the course of its operations.

Our products include various types of jumbo bags, big bags, and bitumen bags in different sizes (500 to 2500 kg), with or without branding, in models with 8 loops, 4 loops, 2 loops, 1 spout top, and 2 spout tops. Additionally, we offer silk nylon at very competitive prices and according to international standards.
